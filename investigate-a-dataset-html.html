No show data set Investigation¶

Introduction¶

CONTEXT: A person makes a doctor appointment, receives all the instructions and no-show. Who to blame?

CONTENT: 300k medical appointments and its 15 variables (characteristics) of each. The most important one if the patient show-up or no-show the appointment.

DATA: PatientId - Identification of a patient AppointmentID - Identification of each appointment Gender = Male or Female Age = How old is the patient. Neighbourhood = Where the appointment takes place. Scholarship = Ture of False . Hipertension = True or False Diabetes = True or False Alcoholism = True or False Handcap = True or False SMS_received = 1 or more messages sent to the patient. No-show = True or False.

Conclusions¶

The aim of the above analysis was to find out reasons why a paitent would not show up to thier appointments The ind

1): It is clear that more number of females and present than males,meaning females are more likely to take better care of their health's,Although the number of females are more both the genders are equally not likely to miss their appointments 2): Interestingly enough the people who received an sms showed up in less numbers than the ones who recevied it 2A): This trend continues onto the genders meaning, that both the genders who did not receive an sms showed up more (almost twice!) that of the one's who recived sms, maybe people are more relaxed when they are reminded of an appointment 3): the paitents who had shcolarship did show up less than the ones who did have it 4): The paitents with diabetes showed up more to their appointments 4A): This could mean that people suffering form dibetes take better care of their health 5): People with hipertention showed up more to their appointments than the ones who did not

LIMITATIONS: this analysis did not perform any Stastistical tests, and the results are based on ratios, Factors such as neighbourhood's were not taken into account, the data was assumed to be accurate after thorough wrangling and inspection,The above are not the only causes other external factors were not taken into account.The corelation dose not always mean causation
